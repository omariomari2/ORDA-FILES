Our Services

Everything you need for safer, smarter rides—plus reliable delivery and hyper‑local news built for your community.

Rides

Order rides in seconds

Tap to request a bike or trike with upfront pricing, live tracking, and safety sharing built in.

  • Enter your destination by typing, tapping the map, or speaking in English or Twi.
  • Choose your ride class (economy motorbike, private car, or shared options) with clear fare estimates up front.
  • Schedule a ride immediately or up to 24 hours in advance.
  • View driver details—photo, vehicle make and plate number—and real-time GPS tracking as soon as your ride is en route.
  • Receive automated SMS and in-app notifications in local languages for driver arrival, trip start, and drop-off confirmation.

Drivers are vetted and trained for rural and peri‑urban roads. Share your ETA and trip status with family even on low connectivity.

Delivery

Deliver anything—fast

Door‑to‑door parcel delivery with verified riders, OTP handoff, and photo proof.

  • Enter pickup and drop-off locations by typing, tapping the map, or speaking in English or Twi.
  • Choose your delivery type—documents, food, packages—and get matched with the right vehicle (motorbike, tricycle, or car) based on size and urgency.
  • Schedule a delivery instantly or up to 24 hours ahead, with flexible time slots for businesses and individuals.
  • View rider details—photo, vehicle type, and plate number—plus real-time GPS tracking from pickup to drop-off.
  • Receive automated SMS and in-app alerts in local languages for pickup confirmation, delivery progress, and successful handoff.

Every step uses low-data design for fast performance on any device, and integrates with mobile-money wallets for instant, cash-free or cash-on-delivery payment.

Local News

Stay ahead with local alerts

Get trusted, hyper‑local updates—road closures, market info, and safety alerts—even offline.

  • Browse headlines in English or Twi, tailored to your region.
  • Get bite-sized updates on traffic, fuel prices, weather, and community alerts.
  • Receive push notifications for urgent news and transport disruptions.
  • Follow verified local sources and citizen reports.
  • Access stories using low-data mode for fast loading.
  • Share updates directly with friends or riders in-app.

We work with community leaders to verify updates quickly so you always know what’s happening around you.
